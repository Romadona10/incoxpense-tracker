Welcome to Romadona-Expense Tracker Here's a quick guide to help you get started

"No matter how much our Income increases we never seem able to keep our spending in life.if we don't manage our money wisely we tend to drift along just hoping for the best."
Our app is designed to make tracking your expenses simple and intuitive. Here are some tips to help you navigate:

  • Dashboard: Provides an overview of your annual expenses, showing a graphical representation of your annual expenses. This helps you identify the category of products and services that consumes a significant part of your budget. It also shows your budget progress and includes a View button that displays the items or services you've spent on. You can also navigate to Monthly Summary (under Dashboard) to view your monthly expenses, categorized by date, category, description, and amount, along with a calculated total for each month.


  • Expenses: Click "Add New Expense" on the Dashboard or navigate to the "Expenses" tab to log a new expense.The expense-list is to give you normal breakdown of the monthly expenses,you could select each month so see the expenses you made per that Month.After purchasing products or services,quickly navigate to the add-expenses,under the expenese tab,and add your expenses,once saved,go back to the dashboard to view it.


  • Categories:Categories are names of products or services that you purchase,eg'food,'health','Entertainment' etc.so when you click on the category tab,you will see the categories-list,add-categories,and edit categories.


  • Reports: This tab,has two parts,namely:Monthly-Report and Export Report.The former gives you a graphical representation of the most expenses-sucking category,and also a summation of the money spent per month.The later aims at exporting the reports through PDF or Excel,based on the user's preference.


  • Settings: Customize your preferences, including currency, notifications, and reminder frequency.


  • Help: Access this guide any time to refresh your knowledge of app features.

Use the navigation bar at the top to quickly access these features and explore more ways to manage your finances!
